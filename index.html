A school only for girls.

Pine Mount school has a motto, "Heavens Light Our Guide". the oldest record of the school dated 1900 but the school has a story even before this date. It is very old. The school has lots of teachers and students.




Picturesque in pine mount school

The school has a large field, a playground, a basketball court, buildings with classrooms, etc. It's clases are from KG to class 12. Each class is divided into 4 sections.
